
Admin panel
===========

Login
*****

By default there is  `admin panel <http://127.0.0.1/admin/>`_ available. The defualt credentials are:

* user: `admin`
* password: `admin`

After login you have access to all models exposed in the `Django admin panel <https://docs.djangoproject.com/en/2.2/ref/contrib/admin/>`_ .
